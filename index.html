FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

What is Flux Systems?

Flux Systems is a Canadian automation company founded by Joel-Andre Bissonnette, a former plumbing business owner with 8+ years in the construction industry. We build voice-powered workflow automation for trades businesses, helping plumbers, electricians, and HVAC contractors eliminate paperwork and get paid faster.

What is Rough-In?

Rough-In is Flux Systems' flagship voice-to-invoice automation platform built specifically for trades. Instead of filling out forms or typing into spreadsheets, you simply speak your job details into Messenger, Telegram, or our webapp. Rough-In automatically captures client information, job descriptions, materials used, labor time, and creates organized job logs with weekly reports. But that's not all—Rough-In also includes an AI assistant plugged directly into Ontario and Quebec building codes. Need to verify a code requirement on-site? Just ask your question and get instant answers without flipping through manuals. No typing. No searching. Just talk.

Who founded Flux Systems?

Flux Systems was founded by Joel-Andre Bissonnette, who spent 8 years running a plumbing business before building Flux Systems. After spending too many nights doing paperwork instead of growing his company, Joel-Andre built Rough-In—the automation tool he wished he had as a trades business owner. Today, Flux Systems combines real construction industry experience with world-class automation expertise to help small businesses save time and make money.

How much does Rough-In cost?

Rough-In is currently in beta with a limited number of early adopters. Our planned pricing is $297 setup fee plus $47 per month, which includes all updates, upgrades, and ongoing support. This investment typically pays for itself in the first month by saving 10-15 hours of administrative time. To discuss early access pricing, book a demo with our team.

Does Flux Systems only work with trades businesses?

While Rough-In was built specifically for trades (plumbing, electrical, HVAC), Flux Systems builds custom workflow automation for businesses in any industry. We specialize in taking repetitive administrative tasks and turning them into voice-activated or automated workflows. Whether you're in construction, retail, services, or any other field, if you're losing time to paperwork, Flux Systems can help. Contact us to discuss your specific automation needs.

How does the building code assistant work?

Rough-In's AI assistant has direct access to Ontario and Quebec building codes, so you can ask questions right from the job site and get instant, accurate answers. For example, you can ask "What's the minimum pipe spacing for hot water lines in Ontario?" or "What's the required ventilation for a bathroom in Quebec?" and get the exact code requirements immediately. No more carrying thick manuals to job sites or googling regulations on your phone. The assistant understands trades terminology and gives you clear, actionable answers based on current building codes.

Is Flux Systems available in both English and French?

Yes! Flux Systems operates bilingually in both English and French. Our founder Joel-Andre Bissonnette is bilingual, and we provide full support in both languages. Whether you prefer to communicate in English or French, Flux Systems can serve your business in the language you're most comfortable with.
